About Me

With over a decade of experience on the front lines of American politics, I founded OLO Group with a single mission: to empower candidates and causes that champion community-first values. My journey began in grassroots organizing, where I learned the profound impact of connecting with voters and understanding their concerns. This foundational experience has shaped my approach to every campaign I’ve managed, from local school board and city council candidates to US Senate, state governor and presidential campaigns. My work spans communities nationwide where I combine clear strategy, data driven field operations and persuasive messaging to deliver results. Years of hands-on experience and a proven record of turning grassroots energy into vote-winning momentum make me a trusted partner for candidates seeking victory. At OLO Group, we don’t just run campaigns; we build movements.
